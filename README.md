# Focus Reader (Fridge Combo #39)

**Focus Reader** is a minimalist "Speed Reading Booster" designed to bypass the fluff and highlight the "bones" of any text. Instead of using AI to summarize, this tool leverages human cognitive patterns to accelerate your reading speed in real-time.

---

### **The "Bone Finder" Logic**
The tool applies two distinct, science-based algorithms to help your brain scan information faster:

* **JP Mode (Particle Filter):** Highlights Kanji, Katakana, numbers, and the last 3 characters of every sentence (the "conclusion"). It dims "particles" (hiragana like は, を, に, の) to surface the core meaning.
* **EN Mode (Bionic Focus):** Highlights the **first 3 characters** of every word. Since the human brain recognizes words by their prefix, your eyes can glide through the text, using the bold parts as "anchors" to reconstruct meaning instantly.

---

### **Features**
* **1 Action / 1 Second:** Paste and click [FOCUS]. No waiting for AI processing.
* **Privacy-First:** Browser-only. Your text never leaves your device.
* **Dual-Language Support:** Optimized for both Japanese and English reading habits.
* **Low-Spec Friendly:** Ultra-lightweight code that runs smoothly on any mobile or desktop browser.

---

### **How to Use**
1.  Paste your long text (blog posts, reports, or emails).
2.  Select **[JP]** or **[EN]** mode.
3.  Click **[FOCUS]**.
4.  Scan the "bones" and feel your brain accelerate.
